Toyota Technology Features.

Remote Air Conditioning

Remote Air Conditioning is probably the best thing to happen ever. As part of the Deluxe Solar Roof packages and Solar Roof packages in the Toyota Prius, Remote Air Conditioning can be activated from up to 30 yards away to cool down your interior for up to 3 minutes. So that short walk from the beach just gained a very cool light at the end of the tunnel.

Smart Key System.

The Smart Key System is available on several Toyota vehicles including the Avalon Hybrid. This Toyota technology is designed to unlock your vehicle and turn on the interior lights from just a few feet away to make getting in much easier. With the push button start/stop, you can get going quickly without the hassle of locating your keys while juggling shopping bags after a Kearney Mesa shopping trip..

Head-Up Display

The Head-Up Display was designed to help you focus on the road while still keeping you in the loop on important information including current speed. This technology is available in the Prius. Head-Up Display projects Hybrid System Indicator, vehicle speed, and navigation information right on your windshield. You can see the information without diverting your eyes from the road.

Dynamic Radar Cruise Control.

Dynamic Radar Cruise Control (DRCC) is an excellent new Toyota technology which utilizes radar technology to automatically adjust your vehicle’s speed to help you maintain a pre-set distance between your vehicle and the vehicle ahead. This technology is especially great for avoiding low-speed collisions when sitting in Escondido traffic. This technology is available in the Toyota Avalon..

Star Safety System.

The Star Safety System is available in many of the Toyota lineup including the Toyota Tundra. The system includes six advanced technology safety features to keep you and your passengers safe. These features include Traction Control (TRAC), Electronic Brake-force Distribution (EBD), Anti-lock Brake System (ABS), Smart Stop Technology (SST), Vehicle Stability Control (VSC), and Brake Assist (BA).

Toyota Care.

Toyota Care might not technically be a Toyota technology, but it is one of the best features of buying a new Toyota vehicle because it helps you make sure that all of your vehicle’s technology is running in tip top shape. Toyota Care is standard on the Toyota Highlander. It covers your vehicle maintenance for 25,000 miles or 2 years, whichever comes first and includes 24-hour roadside assistance for 2 years

Land Cruiser 2022

The 2022 Land Cruiser LC300 is claimed to be an all-new model. The full-size SUV utilises the latest TNGA platform, although the wheelbase mysteriously remains the same. The new Land Cruiser sheds 200 kg from the previous model, which ran for 14 years with numerous facelifts. The interior has been completely revamped as well, although the design remains unremarkable. The screen can be as big as 12.3-inches in the higher trims, while a 9.0-inch screen is offered as standard..
